Projects

1. Navi Mumbai House Price Predection

The aim is to predict the efficient house pricing for real estate customers with respect to their budgets and priorities. Machine Learning, Flask, HTML and CSS used to design and create a scalable end-to-end machine learning pipeline. Requests and Beautiful Soup were used to scrape and parse data from the web. It was regression problem which gave R2 score of 92% after tuning parameters. Heroku used to deploy the project.

2. Directing Customers to Subscription Products through App Behavior Analysis

A fintech company has launched app that has two versions free & paid . The company aims to sell the paid version to the target market to save additional marketing costs. Therefore, they are offered a premium feature of the free version of the program for 24 hours to collect customer behavior. The purpose of this model is to predict which users will not sign up for a paid membership so that more marketing efforts can be made to convert them to paid users.
